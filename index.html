Philosophy

How I Think About AI Security

Security isn't a feature to ship. It's a discipline to build into the fabric of how teams think, design, and operate — especially at the frontier of AI.

01

Attack to Defend

The strongest security programs understand the attacker's perspective. Offensive thinking is not optional — it's the only way to find what defenders overlook.

02

Security by Design

Retrofitting security into AI systems is expensive and incomplete. The architecture conversation must include security from day one, not sprint twenty.

03

Intelligence Drives Precision

Broad defenses without threat intelligence create noise, not safety. Deep understanding of the threat landscape allows for precise, high-impact security investments.

04

People Are the Multiplier

Tools scale tactics. People scale culture. The highest-leverage security investment is building teams who think about risk instinctively, not reactively.
