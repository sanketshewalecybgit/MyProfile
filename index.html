Philosophy

How I Think About Security Operations

Security isn't a checkbox — it's a mindset. The most effective SOC teams aren't just reactive; they're built on intelligence, automation, and an attacker's instinct baked into every workflow.

01

Attack to Defend

The strongest detection logic comes from understanding how attackers think. An offensive foundation isn't optional — it's what separates alert noise from genuine threat coverage.

02

Automate the Repetitive

Manual processes don't scale. Every repeatable security task — scanning, alerting, reporting — should be automated so analysts can focus on what actually requires human judgment.

03

Intelligence Drives Precision

Broad defenses create noise. Threat intelligence — OSINT, MITRE mapping, behavioral context — allows for precise detections that catch real threats without burning out the SOC team.

04

People Are the Multiplier

Tools scale tactics. People scale culture. Investing in mentorship, clear escalation paths, and shared understanding of the threat landscape compounds far beyond any single tool deployment.
